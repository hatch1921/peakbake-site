Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

PeakBake ("the app") is developed and maintained by Frank Hatcher. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data PeakBake collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Location Data

PeakBake requests access to your device's GPS location for the sole purpose of retrieving live atmospheric data, including elevation, humidity, and barometric pressure, from Open-Meteo, a free and open weather API. This data is used exclusively to calculate recipe adjustments.

  • Your location is never stored on our servers.
  • Your location is never shared with third parties.
  • Location data is used in real time and discarded after each session.
  • You may deny location access at any time in your device settings. The app will not function without location access as it is required for atmospheric data retrieval.

Data We Do Not Collect

PeakBake does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, or account credentials
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Recipes saved in My Book are stored locally on your device only

Third-Party Services

PeakBake uses Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to retrieve weather data. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API. Please refer to their privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Data Storage

All recipes saved to My Book are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

PeakBake is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
