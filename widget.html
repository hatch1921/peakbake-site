    How to mark up your ingredients

    Add simple shortcodes alongside your ingredient text. Readers never see them. PeakBake reads them and applies the right adjustments automatically.

    Your recipe post

    How it works

    Three steps, then it runs itself

    📋

    Add two lines to your header

    One CSS line hides the tags from readers. One script tag loads the widget with your key and colors. Works on WordPress, Squarespace, Ghost, and any site where you can edit the header.

    🏷

    Tag your ingredients

    Use the included tag generator to create shortcodes for your ingredient list. Paste them alongside your amounts. Your readers see the original recipe. PeakBake reads the tags invisibly.

    ✓

    Your readers adjust with one click

    A reader in Denver clicks Adjust. Their GPS fires, Open-Meteo pulls live atmospheric data, and the ingredient amounts update instantly. Bake time, temp, and proof adjustments appear automatically.

    Questions

    Frequently asked

    Does it work on WordPress?
    Yes. Paste the two lines into your theme header or use a plugin like Insert Headers and Footers. Full WordPress walkthrough included in the setup manual.
    What does domain-locked mean?
    Your key only works on the domain you register at purchase. If someone copies your embed code it won't work on their site. Your purchase is tied to yourblog.com.
    Can I use it on multiple sites?
    Each purchase covers one domain. If you run multiple recipe sites, purchase once for each. At $4.99 that's still less than a monthly subscription for most tools.
    What if I change my domain?
    Email support@peakbake.com with your old and new domain. We'll update your registration manually. No new purchase needed.
    Do I need to tag every recipe?
    Only recipes you tag will show the Adjust button. Start with your most popular recipes and add more over time.
    Do my readers need to install anything?
    Nothing. Readers click Adjust, grant location permission once, and see their adjusted recipe. No app, no account, no download.
